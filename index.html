My Favorite Jane Austen Things

My first exposure to the work of Jane Austen was when I read the Great Illustrated Classics version of Pride and Prejudice.

The order of publication of Jane Austen's works are as follows:

  1. Sense and Sensibility (1811)
  2. Pride and Prejudice (1813)
  3. Mansfield Park (1814)
  4. Emma (1815)
  5. Northanger Abbey (1817)
  6. Persuasion (1817)
